Tetco – Dubai Client

Developed a complete Sports Event Registration Portal for a Dubai-based client, built with Angular, .NET Core, and SQL Server.
The platform includes dynamic registration forms for Teams, Delegates, and Sponsors, each with its own custom workflow and approval process. I also integrated Stripe for online ticket purchasing and added automated email notifications for registrations and updates.

Event Registration Platform

Developed a scalable event management platform featuring a dynamic drag-and-drop Form Builder for creating custom registration forms with multiple field types.
